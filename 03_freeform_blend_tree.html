What's happening here?
This example demonstrates FreeformBlendTree - a 2D animation blending system where animations can be positioned at arbitrary coordinates in 2D space.
Move the cursor in the square area to blend between:
• Corners: 4 different dance styles
• Center: Idle animation
• Edges/Between: Smooth blends of nearby animations
Perfect for emotion systems, facial expressions, dance/gesture blending, or any scenario requiring natural 2D interpolation between multiple states.
