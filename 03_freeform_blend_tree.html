What's happening here?
This example demonstrates FreeformBlendTree - a 2D animation blending system where animations can be positioned at arbitrary coordinates in 2D space.
• 2D Control: Square area for free movement
• Corner Animations: 4 different styles at corners
• Center Idle: Idle animation at origin
• Smooth Blending: Natural interpolation between nearby states
Perfect for emotion systems, facial expressions, gesture blending, or any scenario requiring natural 2D interpolation between multiple states.
